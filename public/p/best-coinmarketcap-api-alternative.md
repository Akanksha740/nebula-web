## When CMC Falls Short

CoinMarketCap API is designed for portfolio tracking and market overview applications. It lacks the **depth and granularity** needed for:

- Quantitative trading strategy development
- Order book analysis and market microstructure research
- Prediction market backtesting and algo trading
- Liquidity analysis and market making optimization
